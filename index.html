I'm a professional dog photographer and offer home-style dog boarding to one family of dogs at a time. It is important to be that your dog has fun and stays safe while they are with me so that you can enjoy your trip and come home to a happy, tired pup.

I work from home so your dog has the chance to nap, play, walk and hang without the stress of a kennel. I have been working with dogs for over 20 years and have fostered and adopted more than I can count. I have a dog named Moose who is 9 and lazy. He still likes to have short walks but he really prefers couch surfing over long adventures.
